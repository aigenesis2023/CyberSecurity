What It Covers

Three Missions. Three Real Risks.

Mission 01

Sensitive Data Exposure

Employees review an AI chat transcript and identify sensitive data that should never have been shared — credentials, personal information, confidential client details.

Outcome: Know what never to paste into AI tools
Mission 02

AI Hallucination Detection

Employees audit an AI-generated business report against source documents, spotting fabricated statistics, fake citations, and invented facts before they reach stakeholders.

Outcome: Always verify AI output before trusting it
Mission 03

Prompt Injection Attacks

Employees review documents under time pressure and detect hidden malicious instructions embedded in files — learning how attackers manipulate AI systems through poisoned inputs.

Outcome: Spot manipulation before AI acts on it
