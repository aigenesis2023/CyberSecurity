The Problem

Your employees are already using AI.
Most have zero training on the risks.

ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini. Your people are already using them every day. One employee pastes confidential client data into a chatbot and you've got a data breach. An AI makes up a statistic in a board report and nobody catches it. A document with hidden instructions gets fed through your AI tools. This is already happening.

75% of knowledge workers now use AI at work. Nearly half started in the last six months alone. Microsoft Work Trend Index, 2025
$4.44M average cost of a data breach. Unsanctioned AI use was a factor in 1 in 5 breaches. IBM Cost of a Data Breach Report, 2025

What It Covers

Three Missions. Three Real Risks.

Mission 01

Sensitive Data Exposure

Your team reviews an AI chat transcript and spots the sensitive data that should never have been shared. Passwords, personal details, confidential client information.

Outcome: Know what never to paste into AI tools
Mission 02

AI Hallucination Detection

Your team checks an AI-generated business report against the source documents. Can they spot the fabricated statistics and fake citations before it goes to stakeholders?

Outcome: Always verify AI output before trusting it
Mission 03

Hidden AI Manipulation

Documents arrive with hidden instructions buried inside them. Under time pressure, your team has to find the traps before the AI acts on them.

Outcome: Spot manipulation before AI acts on it

Scenarios based on real AI security incidents. Built around risks from the OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications and UK NCSC guidance.
