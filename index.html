AI Safety Training · Learning Objectives
What You Will Learn
This module trains you to identify the three most common AI-related security risks in the workplace. By the end, you will be able to:
01
Identify and classify sensitive data in AI prompts — including credentials, personal data, and internal information — before it leaves your organisation.
02
Spot AI hallucinations in generated reports — fabricated citations, invented statistics, and non-existent legal provisions — before they reach decision-makers.
03
Detect prompt injection attacks hidden inside documents — instructions designed to trick your AI tool into doing something it shouldn't.
